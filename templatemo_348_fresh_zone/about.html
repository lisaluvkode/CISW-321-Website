About Our Company

I am the King of the Tropical Fruit! Let's begin the journey to the Durian King.

Durian King's provide you with all durian fanatics, fruit geeks, horticultural hobbyist, and travelers who love exploring the world through the hunt for durian. We share the love of durians by providing some travel guide and tips in finding a quality durians at a great value. Not only in raw but into a variety of end products including ice cream, snacks, pastries, coffee, tea and milk.

Tours

There are many motivations for traveling the world, getting out of your comfort zones, and experiencing new cultures. Here at Durian King, we're all about hunting out the best durian. Let us help direct you to when, where and how to taste smashingly delicious durian any time of year.

It sounded fun to take a friend out to a place I loved, show them some beautiful views, introduce them to durian farmers and eat my favorite durians together.That’s how I started leading Durian Tours. Soon, I was getting frequent requests from readers of this website to take them with me too. I decided to put together tours to highlight my favorite durian-growing regions, farmers, and of course durians. The first official Durian Tours launched in 2015, and now its like A Thing That Happens Every Year! Life is such a surprise.
